03 · Diagnosis

What's actually slowing the patient down.

10.8MB

Unoptimised images

Three-quarters of the entire site's weight. The single biggest, fastest fix available — and it improves loading speed, mobile ranking, and bounce rate all at once.

2.0s

Server response time

The server takes two full seconds to respond before the page can even start drawing. A hosting/caching fix, not a design one — and it's dragging every other loading metric down with it.

0

Ways to capture a lead outside office hours

Closed Sundays, 1pm finish on Saturdays. Every enquiry outside that window is a phone ringing out or an email sitting unread — no chatbot, no booking widget, no callback form.

5

Missing security headers

No effective CSP, no strong HSTS, no clickjacking protection, no COOP, no Trusted Types. Straightforward to close — the same checklist we run on every client site.

0

Pages with pricing or FAQs

Patients searching "implant cost Sheffield" find nothing on-site and land on a competitor instead.
