All Under One Roof

The Club Facilities

More than a music venue. Crookes Club is a full social hub — something for every mood, every week.

🎵
Concert Room

500-capacity main room with professional PA, stage lighting and full bar. Home to Sheffield's best tribute acts, live bands and comedy nights.

500 capacity
🎱
Snooker & Pool

Two full-sized snooker tables and a pool table, plus active Monday and Wednesday leagues. Serious players welcome, beginners even more so.

Leagues active
🍺
Bar & Lounge

A proper pub bar with a comfortable lounge to match. Real ales, lagers, wines and spirits at prices that make sense. Always welcoming, always good value.

Open daily
🥌
Crown Green Bowls

Join the bowls club and enjoy a classic Sheffield pastime. Coaching available for new members. A genuinely lovely way to spend an afternoon.

Members only
💪
Exercise Classes

Regular fitness classes in our function space. A brilliant extra for members who want to stay active without the gym membership price tag.

Weekly classes
📺
Sports Viewing

Big screens for the big occasions — football, boxing, cricket. The best sport with the best company, right in the heart of Crookes.
